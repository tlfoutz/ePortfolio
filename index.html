Creator and Developer

  • My passion lies in creating. I put the all the effort I can into what I create, because it reflects upon who I am.
  • It is a rare instance where I consider a project to be completely finished. There is always something that can be improved, but I don't let that prevent me from seeing a project to its end.
  • Developing programs and software for other people is an exciting challenge to me. I make sure I keep an open mind when other communicate their ideas and thoughts so I may meet and, hopefully, exceed their expections.

Solution Explorer

  • There is a saying: "There is more than one way to skin a cat." While this is applicable to software engineering, some methods are better than others, given the circumstance. I believe to be worth the effor to take the time to find best path for the situation.
  • Keeping calm has always benefited me when confronted with difficult problems. I have developed multiple practices for maintaining cool-headed, for both myself and encouraging others to do so as well, in high stress level occasions.
  • I find myself playing the devil's advocate when stuck on a problem. In many cases, what I think I know is incorrect, and by challenging myself to determine how I know what I know, I can find where I was wrong.

Seeker of Knowledge

  • I consider myself a perpetual student; not because of how long I have been one, but because I will never stop studying new topics. Whether it be for a do-it-yourself project or a master's degree, I will be a life-long learner.
  • Currently, I am a junior at BYU-Idaho studying Software Engineering with a 3.96 GPA.
  • There is value in studying subjects of all types. Quite often, I come across correlations I do not expect, and then my overall understanding increases.
